Our team

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay is a British celebrity chef, best known for his high-adrenaline cooking shows.
He also owns a chain of restaurants, which have been awarded 17 Michelin stars throughout the years.

One of his most famous signature dishes is his beef Wellington.

Alain Ducasse

Alain Ducasse is a French chef with 21 Michelin stars. He owns over 30 restaurants and founded the Ecole Ducasse culinary school.

One of his signature dishes is the "Cappon Magro", a seafood salad first course based on a 16th century recipe.

Thomas Keller

An American chef known for his precise techniques and high standards, Thomas Keller is the force behind The French Laundry in California, a restaurant celebrated for its exquisite French cuisine and exceptional service.

About Little Salmon

Little Salmon is a cozy family restaurant where the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean come to life.
From freshly grilled seafood to aromatic herbs and sun-kissed vegetables, our dishes are crafted with love and tradition. Whether you're savoring a hearty paella, indulging in homemade hummus, or enjoying a perfectly grilled salmon fillet, every bite feels like a seaside escape.

Little Salmon Restaurant is a harbour of finest tastes!
